The Kitchen Wiz

Scan ingredients. Confirm the list. Cook with what you already have.

Scan

Photo ingredient detection

Users can take or import a kitchen photo so the app can identify visible ingredients from a fridge, cupboard, counter or leftover meal.

Confirm

Human check before recipes

The detected ingredient list is editable, so the recipe engine works from confirmed ingredients rather than pretending the scan is perfect.

Cook

Recipes without a shopping trip

Recipe ideas are built around confirmed ingredients and simple basics, helping reduce food waste and make better use of what is already there.

Positioning

“A practical food-waste and meal-ideas product, not just another recipe app.”

Why it belongs here

Kitchen Wiz extends Mackintosh Projects into useful, public-facing software.

Mackintosh Projects is about practical systems that improve control and reduce waste. Kitchen Wiz applies that same thinking to food at home: better use of existing ingredients, clearer decisions and less unnecessary buying.

Consumer product

A simple app people can try immediately, with a focused promise and clear food-waste angle.

Partnership potential

Useful for food-waste campaigns, kitchen brands, retailers, recipe platforms and grocery services once usage proof is stronger.

Cookbook route

The backend stores public recipe-book candidates so strong recipes can later become a curated cookbook asset.
